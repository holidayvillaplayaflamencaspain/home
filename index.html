Luxurious Holiday Villa

A luxury 3-bedroom detached villa with its own private pool and gardens. The lounge and dining areas are very stylish and furnished with a contemporary look. As owners, we continue to invest year on year in improving the already exceptional high standard villa, so that our holiday guests have everything they need for a perfect home from home holiday in the sun.

Playa Flamenca

The villa is situated in the very popular Playa Flamenca resort near Torrevieja, Costa Blanca. It is just a short walk to the excellent Blue Flag Playa Flamenca Beaches, which are situated between the bays of Punta Prima and La Zenia. Both Beaches are connected by a beautiful promenade, which is impressively landscaped. It has a large open event area with many benches to allow visitors to relax and enjoy the sea views. Visitors can also enjoy a peaceful stroll along the promenade. La Zenia Beach is also a very popular beach. The beach is renowned for its cleanliness and white sand. There are plenty of amenities available including the 4 star Hotel La Zenia.
